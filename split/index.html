01 — Site Purpose

A gathering place for coastal artisan commerce

Manos de Quilla is the Barranquilla Chamber of Commerce site dedicated to small and medium-sized businesses in costume jewelry, weaving, hats, light textile industry, and basic jewelry. The site connects artisans with customers, tourists, and other local brands, offering a business directory, content about the city's culture (Carnival, food, and beach life), and a clear path to joining the Chamber. The goal is to boost the local economy by giving digital visibility to a trade that today is mostly sold face-to-face, on the street and at neighborhood verbenas.

02 — Target Market

Who is the site speaking to?

Artisans and small workshops in Barranquilla and Atlántico who make costume jewelry, weaving, hats, and basic jewelry, along with local buyers, Carnival-season visitors, and urban brands looking to collaborate with coastal talent.

03 — Site Goals

What the site needs to accomplish

Visibility for the artisan

Give every member business a directory listing with direct contact info, without relying only on social media.

New members

Clearly explain the benefits of joining (non-profit, silver, and gold tiers) to attract more workshops and brands.

Cultural identity

Position the Chamber as the trusted voice of artisan commerce tied to Carnival and coastal life.

05 — Scenarios

How each persona uses the site

01

Yesenia registers in the Directory so her workshop appears under the weaving and hats category, uploads photos and contact info, and checks the Chamber's calendar of digital sales workshops.

02

Camilo goes to Join to compare membership tiers, sees that the Silver tier includes a mention on the Chamber's social media and priority for a Carnival booth, and fills out the membership application.

03

Laura visits Discover before her trip to read about Carnival and the artisan districts, then uses the Directory in list view to find jewelry and weaving workshops near her hotel.

06 — SEO Plan

How the site gets found

  • Local keywords in titles, descriptions, and content: "Barranquilla handmade crafts", "Carnival jewelry", "handmade hats Atlántico", "Barranquilla artisan weaving".
  • Verify the site on the Chamber's Google Business Profile.
  • Get inbound links from every member business, craft fairs, and boutique hotels in the city.
  • Embed Google Analytics on all site pages.
  • Publish seasonal content on Discover before and during Carnival, when tourist traffic searching for local crafts peaks.

Handmade jewelry, weaving, and hats

Body text set in Work Sans — clear and comfortable for reading business listings, forms, and longer descriptions.

Space Mono — labels, prices, and data

Note: the headline typeface (Fraunces) gives it an artisanal, characterful touch; Work Sans keeps the rest of the site legible and tidy. If the final project requires a single typeface family, use Work Sans alone and reserve the 600–700 weights for headings.
